discover our story

From Alexandria to Athens, from Beirut to Barcelona, and from Rome to Rabat, the peoples of the Mediterranean (The Meds) share a passion for Seafood, and take pride in their authentic recipes. The recipes were handed down from one generation to another, perhaps with a touch of ingenuity in the ingredients or a touch of creativity in the cooking. At the Meds, we bring the original recipes to perfection with our own interpretation to reinstate the Mediterranean Culinary Art for you to savor.
